Featured Services

PolicyPulse Surveys

End-to-end survey support for public programmes and research in India.

  • QuickPulse (10-14 days): Topline findings and a brief decision note.
  • PolicyPulse (4-6 weeks): Full survey with cleaned dataset and structured report.
  • GovernancePulse (quarterly): Recurring tracker with dashboards.
